10. Design Principles
Minimal

No shadows, no gradients, no decorative elements. Thin borders and whitespace create hierarchy.

Bold Type

Space Grotesk uppercase headlines carry the visual weight. Body text stays quiet.

Generous Space

Sections breathe with 100px+ padding. Content never feels cramped. Mobile scales proportionally.

Purple Sparingly

Purple marks the accent — interactive elements, active states, emphasis. Black is the primary action color.

Warm Neutrals

Stone palette (#fafaf9, #e7e5e4, #a8a29e) instead of cold greys. Feels approachable without being playful.

Pill Everything

Buttons, badges, tags, chips — all pill-shaped (50px radius). Cards use 12-16px radius. Inputs use 10px.
